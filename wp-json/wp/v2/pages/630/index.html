Welcome to our Children’s Ministry at the Seventh-day Adventist church! Our ministry is all about creating a fun, engaging, and nurturing environment where children can grow in their love for Jesus and His teachings. Through interactive lessons, games, music, and crafts, we aim to help children develop a strong foundation of faith while fostering friendships and a sense of belonging in our church family. We believe in the importance of providing age-appropriate resources and experiences that inspire children to discover God’s love for themselves and to live out their faith in meaningful ways. Join us as we journey alongside these young disciples, guiding them on their path of spiritual growth and discovery!<\/span><\/p>\n
