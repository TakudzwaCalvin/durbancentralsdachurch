Welcome to the Women’s Ministry at Durban Central Seventh-day Adventist church! Our ministry is a vibrant community of women who come together to support, uplift, and empower one another in our faith journey. Through regular gatherings, study groups, and outreach initiatives, we seek to deepen our relationship with God and each other. We believe in the importance of nurturing our spiritual, emotional, and physical well-being, and we strive to create a safe and nurturing space where women can grow in their faith, develop lasting friendships, and make a positive impact in our community and beyond. Join us as we journey together in faith, love, and service!<\/span><\/p>\n
